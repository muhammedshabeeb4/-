On Life

~Muhammed Shabeeb

One of the greatest tragedies of human life is that it’s fleeting. Our efforts are futile in the grander scheme of things. Substance is in flux. Our species is corrupt and violent.A small flaw can lead to a whole life unravelling. Some people are swayed by popular opinion while a few assume the role of a trendsetter. Some are fervent about their convictions while a few subject it to scrutiny and scepticism. Some toil every day to make ends meet while a few are born into the riches. In the face of such contradictions, we always pull ourselves up by the bootstraps, have a decent handle over our temper and for the most part cope well against such an absurd background. As Sigmund Freud puts it beautifully ” Love and work. Work and love. That’s all there is”

To live well,don't look for answer.Learn to love the questions.Life should be much more about exploration, wonder, adventure, and reflection rather than evidence, order and direction

Don't give up on the spirtual advantages of self-sufficiency.nowadays, an "ordinary" life is extraordinary
