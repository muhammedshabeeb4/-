On Love

~Muhammed Shabeeb

It’s the epicentre of our being. All that we do is directed to this same end. In it’s presence serenity runs high and it’s absence festers decaying wounds. If one were to associate love to anything it’d closely resemble a lottery ticket. Keeps a person brimming with hope so much so that even a herculean task seems trifling. All our longings will be just a hair’s breadth away. Maybe this infantile optimism is what is secretly carving an ever expanding chasm of darkness. One must instead think of love as the air inhaled. Can’t claim authority yet it’s a part of you. Fleeting yet quite literally life-giving. Intangible but when sweet the fabric of the universe can be felt between the fingers. What an elusive entity love is. This intricate web of ambiguity can be untangled once a feeling of quietude overtakes the whirl of the soul and just sink into the touch of the wind and realise without inhibition that it never ceases to exist. So, just keep breathing.
